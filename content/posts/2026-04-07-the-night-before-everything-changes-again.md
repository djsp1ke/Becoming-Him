---
title: The night before everything changes again
summary: A short reflection on the moments before a new beginning.
date: 2026-04-07
---

The night before everything changes again is one of the quietest times.

It is when the noise of the day fades and your mind begins to move forward.

- Breathe slowly
- Write down your next step
- Trust the process

The next morning will arrive with new clarity, and what feels uncertain tonight can feel certain tomorrow.
